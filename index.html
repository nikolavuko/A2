HISTORY OF MEN'S TENNIS

Since the beginning of the open era (1968), tennis has exploded in popularity as a televised sport and competition. Today we will dive into the history of the men's side of tennis, seeing what countries have had the most success, the diversity of the surfaces on tour, and delving deeper into the infamous “Big 3”.

In the open tour, different tournaments have different worth, ranging from tournaments that are worth 250 points all the way to the most pristine “Grand Slams”, worth 2000 points. When looking into the history of a player's career, all wins are important, but the Grand Slams are what is looked at the most.
